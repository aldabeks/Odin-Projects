Chicken Rice Porridge (Chicken Arroz Caldo)

About Chicken Rice Porridge

Arroz caldo is a comforting and flavorsome Filipino rice and chicken soup served mainly as a snack but can be enjoyed any time of the day. It's mainly made with chicken and rice. And flavored with garlic, onions, ginger, fish sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 (2 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 ¼ pounds chicken wings
  • 5 ¼ cups chicken broth
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook and stir onion, garlic, and ginger in hot oil until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken wings, cook and stir for 1 minutes.
  3. Pour chicken broth into the pot. Stir in sweet rice and bring mixture to a boil.
  4. Cover and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent rice from sticking to the bottom of the pot.
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Serve with lemon slices.
