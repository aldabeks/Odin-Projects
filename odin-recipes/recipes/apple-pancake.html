German Apple Pancake

About Apple Pancake

This German apple pancake is also known as a Dutch baby. It is a terrific baked pancake recipe for those special occasions. If you are strapped for time, you can start it the evening before and pretty much pop it in the oven. Delicious topped with powdered sugar, maple syrup, or a dollop of yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ white sugar

Instructions

  1. Combine eggs, flour, 1 tablespoon sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Gradually mix in milk, stirring constantly. Add vanilla, melted butter and 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg.
  3. Let batter stand for 30 minutes
  4. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
  5. Brush the sides of a pan, put all the ingredient in the pan.
  6. Place pan over medium heat until mixture bubbles.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.
  8. Slice pancake onto a serving platter and cut into wedges.
